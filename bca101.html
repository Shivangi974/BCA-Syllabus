SEMESTER 1

BCA-101: MATHEMATICAL FOUNDATION

Unit-1: Differential Calculus

Successive Differentiation, Leibnitz Theorem, Expansion of function of one variable in Taylor's and McLaurin's infinite series, Maxima and minima of function of one variable, Partial Derivatives, Euler's theorem, Change of variables, Total differentiation, Taylor's series in two variables, Maxima and minima of two variables.

Unit-2: Integral Calculus

Definite integral and its application for area, length, and volume, Multiple integrals, Change of order of integration.

Unit-3: Differential Equation

First degree and first order differential equation, Higher order differential equation with constant coefficients, Linear partial differential equation of first order, P.D.E. of higher orders with constant coefficients.

Unit-4: Matrix Algebra

Elementary transformation, Inverse of a matrix by row operation, Rank, Solution of a system of linear simultaneous equations by matrix method, Eigenvalue and Eigenvector, Cayley-Hamilton theorem, Quadratic forms.
